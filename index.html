Methodology & Notes
  • Speediance Gym Monster 2 (Average): Avg of lowest SKU (Basic, $3,749) and highest standard SKU (Family Plus, $4,499) at regular MSRP per speediance.com → $4,124. Wellness+ subscription ($24.99/mo, sale $19.99) is optional — machine works fully without it.
  • Peloton Bikes (Average): Avg of new Cross Training Bike ($1,695) and Bike+ ($2,695) → $2,195. Refurbished SKUs excluded.
  • Hydrow Rowers (Average): Avg of Wave ($1,995), Origin ($2,195), Arc ($2,295) → $2,162. Can use "Just Row" mode without subscription; $50/mo or $600/yr prepaid (no actual discount).
  • Ergatta Rowers (Average): Avg of Luxe ($2,499) and Lite ($1,699) → $1,949. Has bundle discounts: 1yr $390 (saves $78), 3yr $1,044 (saves $360), 5yr $1,620 (saves $720).
  • Tempo (Average): Avg of lowest bundle ($59/mo Y1) and highest bundle ($159/mo Y1) per tempo.fit/shop → $109/mo Y1, $39/mo Y2+. Has 12 & 24 mo prepaid plans (savings, exact discount not publicly disclosed). Equipment paid over 12 months, yours to keep after.
  • Peloton All-Access: $49.99/mo (raised from $44 in October 2025). No public annual prepaid or bundle discounts.
  • Peloton Bike+ Rental: $150 setup + $125/mo all-inclusive — no separate membership required.
  • Hydrow LYQUID: Subscription required to operate the machine. $50/mo or $600/yr (no discount).
  • Eight Sleep Pod 5 Core: Annual billing only — Standard $199/yr, Enhanced $299/yr, Elite $399/yr. No monthly billing option.
  • WHOOP (Average): Avg of all 3 membership tiers: One ($199/yr) + Peak ($239/yr) + Life ($359/yr) → $265.67/yr Year 1 = $22.14/mo equivalent. Device hardware bundled with subscription on all tiers; no separate hardware cost. All tiers HSA/FSA eligible.
  • Tonal 2: Monthly only at $59.95/mo, 12-mo lock-in. No bundle/prepaid discount available.
  • Bundle-discount summary: Only Ergatta and Tempo offer multi-year prepaid discounts. Ergatta is the most aggressive (up to $720 savings on 5yr). Tonal, Peloton, Hydrow, and Eight Sleep have no meaningful prepaid discounts.

Apps Methodology & Notes
  • Apple Fitness+: Single tier — $9.99/mo or $79.99/yr ($6.67/mo annual, ~33% savings). Requires iPhone, optional Apple Watch.

  • Headspace: $12.99/mo or $69.99/yr ($5.83/mo annual, ~55% savings). Family plan ($99.99/yr for 6 accounts) and Lifetime ($399.99) excluded.
  • Calm Premium: $14.99/mo (Apple) or $69.99/yr (Apple) / $79.99/yr (web) → using $5.83/mo annual rate. Family ($99.99/yr) and Lifetime ($399-499) excluded.
  • Noom Weight: $59/mo monthly or $17.42/mo on 12-mo annual plan (~70% savings — steepest discount in the set). Excludes Noom Med (GLP-1 program $69-$279/mo).
  • Peloton App (Average): Avg of App One ($12.99) + App+ ($28.99) → $20.99/mo. Both raised in October 2025. Free tier excluded.
  • iFit (Average): Avg of Train ($15/mo individual) + Pro ($39/mo family of 5) → $27/mo. Annual rate: $144 + $396 → effective $22.50/mo.
  • Centr: $29.99/mo, $79.99/quarter, or $179.99/yr ($15/mo annual, ~50% savings). Single subscription tier; quarterly tier excluded from chart.

  • Trainwell: $149/mo, billed in 3-month increments. Includes dedicated 1-on-1 coach with unlimited messaging/video.
  • Future (Average): Avg of Standard coach ($149/mo) + Pro coach ($199/mo) → $174/mo. Both include dedicated 1-on-1 coaching with daily check-ins.
  • Totals calculation: Uses monthly subscription rate to allow direct apples-to-apples comparison; annual savings shown separately in Annual / Promo column.
Key Takeaways — Fitness Apps Landscape Pricing patterns and competitive positioning
Mindfulness Tier

Headspace & Calm ($69.99/yr)

Identical $69.99/yr annual pricing — both at $5.83/mo annual, the cheapest premium tier. Calm's monthly is $14.99, Headspace's is $12.99. Mindfulness is the most price-aggressive app vertical.

Best Annual Discount

Noom & Ladder/Centr (~50–70% off)

Noom drops $59 → $17.42/mo on annual (~70% off). Ladder Pro drops $29.99 → $14.99/mo and Centr $29.99 → $15/mo (both 50% off annual). All three lean heavily on annual retention.

Premium Hardcap

Future ($174/mo avg)

Most expensive in the comparison. Standard coach $149/mo, Pro coach $199/mo. Includes dedicated 1-on-1 trainer with daily text check-ins. Closer to in-person training pricing than app-tier.

Hidden 3-Month Lock

Trainwell ($149/mo, 3-mo billing)

Only app in this set with a multi-month billing commitment — billed in 3-month increments at $447 upfront. Comparable to Future's pricing but bundled differently.

App vs. 1-on-1 Coaching

~5x Price Gap

Pure content apps (Apple Fitness+, Strava, Peloton, Centr) cluster $7–$30/mo. Coaching apps (Trainwell, Future) jump to $149–$199/mo. The middle ground from $30–$150 is largely empty.

Family-Optimized

iFit Pro Family ($39/mo, 5 users)

$7.80/user/mo at the family tier — best-in-class for households. Pairs natively with NordicTrack/ProForm/Freemotion equipment. iFit Train ($15/mo) covers app-only individual.
